## Voice & Tone

### We Are
- **Optimistic** - Solarpunk is hopeful futurism
- **Technical** - We respect engineering precision
- **Human-centered** - Technology serves people
- **Sustainable** - Long-term thinking over quick wins

### We Avoid
- Hype and buzzwords
- Fear-based messaging
- Over-promising
- Corporate jargon

### Example Messaging

✅ "Blueprint helps you automate the tedious so you can focus on living."

❌ "Revolutionary AI-powered synergistic automation solutions."

## Visual Elements

### Imagery Style
- Nature + technology fusion
- Clean, minimal interfaces
- Warm lighting (solar aesthetic)
- Organic shapes with precise geometry

### Iconography
- Line icons (Lucide)
- Rounded corners
- Consistent stroke width
- Teal/emerald/gold accents

### Effects
- Glass morphism (blur + transparency)
- Subtle gradients (teal → emerald → gold)
- Bioluminescent glows
- Beam animations

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo
- Primary: "zer0" wordmark
- Secondary: "zer0 | Blueprint" lockup
- Always use on dark backgrounds

### Spacing
- Generous whitespace
- Consistent padding (multiples of 4px)
- Breathing room around elements

### Accessibility
- All color combinations meet WCAG AA
- Focus states visible
- Reduced motion support

For questions about brand usage, refer to the full color palette specification or website implementation.
